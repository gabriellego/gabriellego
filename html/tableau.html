Profit by State

California and New York by far have the highest profit. Many have relatively low profit or negative profit. No state's profit is super close to the two highest of California and New York.



CA Profit

California profit over the 2 year span of 2012-2014 seemed to jump greatly high to low. Profit at the beginning of 2012 was significantly higher than the massive drop at the end of 2014. But comparitively speaking, California profit is much higher than most states in the U.S.



CA Product Category Profit

Within California, the highest profit category is that of office supplies. Technology is pretty close to office supplies, but furniture is significantly lower compared to both technology and office supply profits.



Forecasted Sales for Office Supplies in CA

Forecasted sales in CA for office supplies seem as though it will continue to be profitable as increase is expected in the following months.
